IDEX
ZINC token is a cryptoasset which is inherently volatile. There are no guarantees over the cost of services which will be redeemable with ZINC tokens on the Zinc platform.

Token usages

Messaging

Zinc will be used by companies or recruiters in order to contact workers in the network and in order to request sharing work profiles. Workers can earn this Zinc fee by replying and sharing their Zinc profile. Zinc becomes the medium of exchange for sharing work/skills based data.

Recruiting

Zinc is the sole payment method for companies and recruiters who wish to screen prospective workers through our referencing dApp.

Identity contracts

Zinc tokens will fund the creation of a decentralized identity contract holding their Zinc profile and any other identity claims they wish to hold live on the mainnet. This could be used as your decentralised ID wallet for crowdsales, business transactions or work.

Voting rights

Zinc token gives you voting rights in all decisions governed by the Zinc foundation. Anyone who has a stake in Zinc has an influence on the decentralised organisations votes.
